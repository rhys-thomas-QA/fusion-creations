Why Use Them

Badges that feel part of the event, not an afterthought

Standard plastic sleeves are fine when you only need something temporary. Custom event badge holders work better when the badge is part of the experience: a conference, company day, partner event, staff rollout, or branded onboarding pack.

Because each holder is 3D printed, names, roles, colours, and company details can change from person to person without needing a separate mould or bulk print run for every design.

Conferences

Partner events

Add company names, partner details, or attendee groups without making every holder identical.

Onboarding

FAQ

Event badge holder questions

Can every holder have a different name?

Yes. Each event badge holder can include a different name, company, department, team colour, role, or attendee type.

How much do event badge holders cost?

Logo-only holders start from £5.50 per unit. Holders with individual names or other personalisation start from £6 per unit. Final pricing depends on quantity, colours, logo detail, and deadline.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most event badge holder orders start from around 50 units, although I can discuss smaller sample or trial runs depending on the design.

How long do they take to produce?

Lead time depends on quantity and design complexity. I can often produce smaller batches in 1 to 2 weeks. Larger personalised event orders can need several weeks.
