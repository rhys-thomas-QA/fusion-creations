FAQ

Custom ID card holder questions

Can each holder have a different name?

Yes. Each holder can have a different name, department, role, team colour, or company name. This is where 3D printing is especially useful because personalisation does not need a separate mould for every design.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most business orders start from around 50 units, though smaller sample or trial runs can be discussed depending on the design.

Can you match our brand colours?

Yes. Send over brand colours or reference files and Fusion Creations can suggest the closest practical 3D printing colours for the body, logo, and text details.

How long does production take?

Lead time depends on quantity, design complexity, and deadline. Smaller batches can often be produced in 1 to 2 weeks. Larger personalised batches usually need several weeks.

Do you ship across the UK?

Yes. Fusion Creations is UK-based and can ship completed holders across the UK.
