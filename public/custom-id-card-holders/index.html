FAQ

Custom ID card holder questions

How much do custom ID card holders cost?

Logo-only holders start from £5.50 per unit. Holders with individual names or other personalisation start from £6 per unit. Final pricing depends on quantity, colours, logo detail, and deadline.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most business orders start from around 50 units, though I can discuss smaller sample or trial runs depending on the design.

Can each holder have a different name?

Yes. Each holder can have a different name, department, role, team colour, or company name. This is where 3D printing works well because personalisation does not need a separate mould for every design.

What logo file do you need?

A vector file such as SVG, AI, EPS, or PDF is best. A clear PNG can also work for an early quote. If the logo is very detailed, I will let you know what may need simplifying for a clean print.

Can you match our brand colours?

Yes. Send over brand colours or reference files and Fusion Creations can suggest the closest practical 3D printing colours for the body, logo, and text details.

How long does production take?

Lead time depends on quantity, design complexity, and deadline. I can often produce smaller batches in 1 to 2 weeks. Larger personalised batches can need several weeks.

What happens after I submit the form?

I will reply with the best next step. A rough quantity, logo, preferred colours, deadline, and whether each holder needs a name is enough to start. If the job looks right, I will send a fuller production details form before anything is made.
