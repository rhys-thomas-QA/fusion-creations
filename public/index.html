Why This Product

Why custom ID card holders work

I'm Rhys, and I run Fusion Creations from the UK. The business now centres on custom ID card holders and branded badge holders for teams that want something more polished than a generic plastic sleeve.

Every holder is 3D printed to order using durable PLA and PETG plastics. Colours, logos, text, individual names, team labels, and event details can be built into the holder so the finished badge feels like part of your brand.

The 3D printed process is especially useful for personalised batches because there are no expensive mould tools. Whether you need 50 holders for an event or 1,000 for a company rollout, the design can stay flexible and the pricing remains sensible.

I've worked with teams at American Express, ClearScore, and DriveScore, delivering holders for internal events, onboarding packs, and everyday office use. For more detail, see the custom ID card holders service page.

Common Questions

Custom ID card holder FAQ

What are custom 3D printed ID card holders?
They are badge holders made around your company colours, logo, and employee names, so you are not limited to plain plastic sleeves.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Most jobs start at around 50 units, although smaller sample runs can be discussed. The largest single order so far was 820 units for ClearScore, delivered in 6 weeks.
How long does delivery take?
Turnaround depends on order size. Small orders (under 100 units) typically take 1 to 2 weeks. Larger orders of 500 or more units usually take 4 to 6 weeks. We are UK-based and ship across the country.
What materials are used?
The holders are 3D printed using PLA and PETG plastics. They are lightweight, durable, and available in a wide range of colours, including translucent and textured finishes.
Can I customise the colours and add my company logo?
Yes. You can choose the holder colour, text colour, company logo, and employee name. Use the online designer tool to preview the holder in 3D before ordering.
How much do custom ID card holders cost?
Logo-only holders start from £5.50 per unit. Holders with individual names or other personalisation start from £6 per unit. Final pricing depends on quantity, colours, logo detail, and deadline.
