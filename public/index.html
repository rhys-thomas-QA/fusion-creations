About

Why 3D Printed Badge Holders?

I'm Rhys, and I run Fusion Creations from the UK. What started as a hobby with a 3D printer turned into a business when a mate asked me to make branded badge holders for his company. Since then, I've delivered thousands of custom holders to businesses across the country.

Every holder is 3D printed to order using durable PLA and PETG plastics. Unlike generic lanyards or flimsy plastic sleeves, these are solid, branded items your team will actually want to use. Each one can be fully customised with your company colours, logo, and individual employee names.

3D printing means there are no tooling costs or massive minimum orders. Whether you need 50 holders for a new starter kit or 1,000 for the whole office, the per-unit cost stays reasonable and turnaround is fast. Most orders ship within 1 to 6 weeks depending on quantity.

I've worked with companies like American Express, ClearScore, and DriveScore, delivering holders for internal events, onboarding packs, and everyday office use. If you want something that looks professional and represents your brand properly, get in touch.

Common Questions

FAQ

What are custom 3D printed ID card holders?
Custom 3D printed ID card holders are bespoke badge holders made using 3D printing technology. Each holder can be personalised with your company colours, logo, and employee name, making them a branded alternative to generic lanyards and plastic sleeves.
What is the minimum order quantity?
We typically work with orders from 50 units upwards, though we can accommodate smaller runs for samples or trials. Our largest single order to date was 820 units for ClearScore, delivered in 6 weeks.
How long does delivery take?
Turnaround depends on order size. Small orders (under 100 units) typically take 1 to 2 weeks. Larger orders of 500 or more units usually take 4 to 6 weeks. We are UK-based and ship across the country.
What materials are used?
Our holders are 3D printed using PLA and PETG plastics, which are durable, lightweight, and available in a wide range of colours. We can also produce translucent and textured finishes for a premium look.
Can I customise the colours and add my company logo?
Yes. Every holder is fully customisable. You choose the holder colour, text colour, company logo, and employee name. Use our online designer tool to preview your holder in 3D before ordering.
How much do custom ID card holders cost?
Pricing depends on order quantity, customisation level, and finish. Get in touch via our contact form or email contact.fusioncreations@gmail.com for a free quote.
