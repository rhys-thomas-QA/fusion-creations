What can be customised?

  • Holder body colour.
  • Logo and text colour.
  • Individual names, roles, departments, or attendee types.
  • Company, partner, or event details.
  • Lanyard hole, ring, or attachment position where practical.

What changes the quote?

  • Quantity and number of unique designs.
  • Logo detail and number of colours.
  • Deadline and delivery address.
  • Whether names come in a tidy spreadsheet.
  • Any design changes after sign-off.

What to send for a useful first quote

  • Approximate quantity.
  • Event date or deadline.
  • Logo file, ideally SVG, AI, EPS, or PDF.
  • Brand colours or reference images.
  • Whether each holder needs a name.
  • Example name, role, or department format.
  • Delivery postcode.
  • Whether you want a paid sample holder first.
