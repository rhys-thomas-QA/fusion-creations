Privacy & Cookies

Privacy & cookie information

Last updated 29 May 2026

Who We Are

Fusion Creations makes custom 3D printed ID card holders and related printed products for businesses. You can contact us at contact.fusioncreations@gmail.com.

Information You Send Us

When you submit the quote form, we collect the name, email address, company name, project type, approximate quantity, deadline or event date, personalisation choice, and message you provide so we can respond to your enquiry. The form may also include the page URL, referrer, UTM parameters, and Google click ID where available, so we can understand which marketing activity led to the enquiry.

Optional Cookies And Google Measurement

We use Google tag services for Google Ads, conversion measurement, and analytics-style measurement. These services can use cookies or similar storage to understand visits, quote enquiries, and advertising performance.

If you accept optional cookies, we may send your email address to Google tag services for Enhanced Conversions when you submit the quote form. This helps match a quote enquiry to an ad interaction. If you reject optional cookies, we keep Google advertising, analytics, user-data, and personalisation consent set to denied and do not send the quote form email into the Enhanced Conversions flow.

Necessary Local Storage

We store your cookie choice in your browser so the banner does not reappear on every page. This choice is stored for up to 180 days unless you clear your browser data or change your choice below.

Change Cookie Settings

You can change your optional cookie choice at any time.

How Long We Keep Enquiries

We keep enquiry details for as long as needed to respond, manage the potential order, maintain business records, and understand marketing performance. You can ask us to delete your enquiry details by emailing us.
