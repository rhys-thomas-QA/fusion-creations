# Fusion Creations Weekly Marketing Review

Use this once a week, ideally Monday morning. Keep it to 20 to 30 minutes unless something clearly needs work.

## 1. Google Ads

- Date range: last 7 days and last 30 days.
- Check spend, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, and search terms.
- Add relevant search terms as exact or phrase match when they show buyer intent.
- Add negatives for searches that clearly don't fit the product.
- Leave broad match alone until the campaign has more conversion data.
- Note any term that gets clicks but no enquiry after 10 or more clicks.

## 2. Landing Page

- Check the ad landing page in Clarity.
- Watch 3 to 5 recordings from paid traffic.
- Look for people who stop before the quote form, rage click, or leave after pricing.
- If a pattern repeats, write one specific fix.

## 3. Search Console

- Date range: last 28 days.
- Check queries with impressions and low CTR.
- Check pages getting impressions.
- Add new content ideas only when the query matches a real buyer problem.
- Request indexing for new or updated pages once the page is live.

## 4. Leads and Replies

- Count website enquiries.
- Count n8n outbound drafts created.
- Count replies, positive replies, and no-thanks replies.
- Save any useful customer wording in a notes file for future ads and page copy.

## 5. Next Actions

Pick no more than 3 actions for the week:

- One ad account change.
- One website or landing page change.
- One outreach or content change.

## Current Guardrails

- Keep the main quote form simple.
- Use the production details form only after someone has shown interest.
- Keep copy in British English.
- Avoid em dashes and obvious AI phrasing.
- Don't increase ad budget until search terms and conversion tracking look healthy.
